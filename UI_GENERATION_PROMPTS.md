# UI Generation Prompts for Digital Pharmacy Platform

Here are 3 distinct prompts you can use with AI image generation models (like Midjourney v6, DALL-E 3, or UI-specific models like Uizard) to explore different design directions for your redesign.

## Option 1: The "Modern Professional" (Evolution of Current)
*Focus: Trust, Clarity, Efficiency. Best for a standard medical application.*

> **Prompt:**
> High-fidelity web dashboard UI design for a Digital Pharmacy Platform. The aesthetic is professional, clinical, and trustworthy. **Color Palette:** Primary Deep Teal (#1D857E) and Crisp White, with Soft Blue (#52A8DD) accents. **Layout:** A clean 3-column grid. Left sidebar navigation. Center area showing "Active Prescriptions" cards with status indicators (Pending, Approved) and a "Refill Now" button. Right side panel showing a "Shopping Cart" summary and "Pharmacist Chat" widget. **Style:** Modern clean typography (Roboto/Inter), generous whitespace, subtle drop shadows on cards, rounded corners (8px). Professional medical software, Dribbble trending, 4k resolution.

## Option 2: The "Warm Holistic" (Lifestyle/Wellness)
*Focus: Comfort, Accessibility, Natural. Best for a consumer-facing health brand.*

> **Prompt:**
> UI design concept for a Wellness and Pharmacy application. The vibe is warm, organic, and inviting, moving away from sterile clinical looks. **Color Palette:** Cream/Beige background (#F5F1E6), Sage Green, and Terracotta text. **Typography:** Elegant Serif headings (Lora) paired with soft Sans-serif body. **Key Visuals:** A product browsing page for vitamins and supplements. Product cards have soft, large rounded corners (24px) and flat illustrations instead of generic stock photos. An "AI Health Companion" bubble floats in the corner. **Style:** Minimalist, lifestyle-oriented, soft textures, flat design, aesthetically pleasing, accessible UI.

## Option 3: The "Future Tech" (AI-Centric)
*Focus: Innovation, Speed, Data. Best for highlighting the AI features.*

> **Prompt:**
> Futuristic UI interface for an AI-powered Smart Pharmacy. **Theme:** Dark Mode with Glassmorphism. **Color Palette:** Deep Midnight Blue background, Glowing Neon Teal and Electric Blue gradients. **Layout:** A central conversational interface (AI Health Assistant) that dominates the screen, surrounded by floating semi-transparent glass widgets displaying "Health Vitals", "Order Tracking" (with a progress map), and "Medication Schedule". **Style:** Tech-forward, sleek, frosted glass effects, glowing borders, high contrast, data visualization, sci-fi medical dashboard, high-end fintech style.

---

### Tips for Generation:
*   **Aspect Ratio:** If generating for desktop, add `--ar 16:9` (Midjourney) or specify "Wide desktop view".
*   **Refinement:** If you like a layout but not the colors, try appending "Use the color palette: #1D857E, #F5F1E6, #0A3A37" to the end of the prompt.
